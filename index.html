Apps

A collection of apps by Tim Heuer

Zilch

A strategic card game where the goal is simple: score the lowest. Flip, swap, and reveal your way to victory against the bot.

Sidewall

Precision tire pressure calculator for cyclists, helping you dial in ride-ready PSI for your bike, setup, and conditions.

TrueUp

Expense splitting made simple, helping friends settle shared costs and keep group spending fair without the awkwardness.

Shiplet

Track all your packages in one beautiful app with real-time updates, multi-carrier support, and a delightful experience.

Crown Climb

A single-player offline card game of rank and strategy where you climb the social ladder from peasant to king.

MyWattz

Connect to your Bluetooth cycling sensors and view live power, cadence, and heart rate metrics on iPhone.

PC Screenshotter

Capture screenshots on your Windows PC remotely from your iPhone with a private, local-first setup.

How to Play

Simple rules, deep strategy. Learn in minutes, master over time.

Setup

Each player gets 8 face-down cards in a 4x2 grid. Two cards are revealed at the start. Goal: lowest score wins!

Your Turn

Draw from the deck or take the top discard card. Then swap it with one of your cards or discard and reveal.

Card Values

Cards 0-11 score their face value. Special -5 cards reduce your score. Zero cards are your best friends!

Reveal Strategy

If you discard without swapping, you must reveal one of your hidden cards. sometimes that's exactly what you want.

End Round

When one player reveals all cards, each opponent gets one final turn. Then all cards flip and scores are tallied.

Winning

Play multiple rounds. The player with the lowest cumulative score across all rounds wins the game!
